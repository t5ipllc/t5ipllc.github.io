About T5IP

Although we've helped to develop internet companies since 2000, we began hands-on development in 2012. During that time we've refined our development process to its current levels of excellence and dedication.

We know that our clients choose their consultants and business partners very carefully and, likewise, we choose our partners and clients equally carefully. There must be a fit between what we offer and what you need, and only if there is a fit will we suggest a business relationship.

We offer two service models:

  1. Work for hire: You hire us to develop your website or app.
  2. Funding & development partnership: For select ideas and projects, we will bear the cost of development ourselves, in return for a percentage of equity in the venture. We've brought entire ideas to market, allowing the founder to focus on the idea and marketing and we handle all of the development.
