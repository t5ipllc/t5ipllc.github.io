DEVELOPMENT PROJECTS

Most of our business is with repeat clients. For our development projects we require an upfront deposit and then progress billing at pre-agreed milestones. We review all of our work with our clients in periodic videoconferences where we can demo the project in real time via screen shares. Thus, the highest level of client satisfaction is assured.

ONGOING SUPPORT

For each project, we also enter into a monthly support agreement where we agree to monitor server uptime/availability, monitor for security risks, and provide minor tweaks and edits without entering into additional services contracts or “change orders.” The fee for the monthly service is agree upon in advance and then billed monthly in a “subscription” arrangement.

PRIVACY AND DATA SECURITY

We take privacy very seriously, both in how we build projects and also with our client's sensitive data. We build security and data privacy into every app, and for our own clients, we collect only the data that we absolutely need, keep it secure, and securely delete it when no longer needed. As an example, for payments, we do not collect or store credit card information. Our payment processing is done by third-parties who specialize in providing secure transaction processing and we don't collect or store client's card information at all.
